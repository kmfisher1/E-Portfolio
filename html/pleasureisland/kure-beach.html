Outdoor Revival

Kure Beach, NC is a treasure trove of outdoor adventure and joy. It allows you to reconnect with what really matters, the world around us. Take a break from the technological age, and enjoy the simplpater things in life here in Kure Beach, NC. From walking the trails encircling a Civil War Fort at Fort Fisher, to checking out the local wildlife at the Aquarium, there is something for everyone here at Kure Beach.

Pier and Beachfront Sights

Kure Beach has a number of family friendly attractions for both young and old. From adventuring around the Fort Fisher State Recreation Area, to wetting a line at the Kure Beach Pier, there is something for all the different folks in your life.

Music, Food, And More

Throughout the year, tourists at Kure Beach are thrilled with the different restaurants and live music venus to be found across the town. Dance over to the Ocean Front Park from May to October for the Boogie in the Park concert series, and then grab a bite to eat at one of the many indoor, or outdoor dining restaurants all over the town.
