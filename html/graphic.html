Social Media Marketing Series (1 of 5)

First of a set of 5 images created for Nevada Healthlink's Instagram account. Style and Colors coincide with branded design elements. We were supposed to highlight how easy Nevada Health Link was to work with. The image shows a friendly call center staff waiting for members questions.

Social Media Marketing Series (2 of 5)

Second of a set of 5 images created for Nevada Healthlink's Instagram account. Style and Colors coincide with branded design elements. I enjoyed making these because I got to make a series of similiar designed images based on a central theme.

Social Media Marketing Series (3 of 5)

Third in a set of five images. This image is to show that the customer can be protected, and protect their families/loved ones with Nevada Healthlink. I didn't use a color overlay on this image because I thought the natural colors added to the image's appeal.

Social Media Marketing Series (4 of 5)

Fourth image in this series. A small business owner making a pitch in a meeting. Images on Instagram are usually developed at a 1080px x 1080px size. Further development of this series could be to include links to materials from the Nevada Healthlink website in tags on Instagram.

Social Media Marketing Series (5 of 5)

Fifth image in this series. Barbers are iconic examples of self employeed or small business owners. This post is to reach out to small business owners to let them know Nevada Healthlink can help them with their insurance needs.
