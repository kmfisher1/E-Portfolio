Ken Fisher

Greetings and Welcome! My name is Ken Fisher. I'm a User Experience and Design student at WakeTech Community College in Raleigh, NC. I'm also the owner/operator of Ken Fisher Designs, a up and coming graphic design and web design studio based out of Fuquay Varina, NC.

I have a long love of HTML and CSS that has started back in the late 90s, when I was in high school. The Internet was a relatively new social construct at the time, and I found that I enjoyed designing and marking my little corner of it. I went to college in the early 2000's for English with a focus on secondary education because, at the time, I had toyed with the idea of being a public school teacher. It didn't take long for me to figure out that path was not for me.

While I was in college at the University of Texas @ Arlington, I was approached for the founder of a student group called PAWS (Pet and Animal Wellness Society) to design the first iteration of their webpage. Even though I didn't work in the IT field, or with HTML, I have spent the last 20 years keeping in touch with HTML/CSS. I've been back to WakeTech in the interim to just keep up to date with the newest best practices and trending fads.

My love of graphic design started while I was in college as well. A past time I developed was centered around creative writing. I found that I wanted to create my own edited images to go along with my written passages, and soon learned to edit images on a program called Microsoft Photodraw. Since I've taken classes on Illustrator and Photoshop.

Aside from my academic pursuits, I have a strong belief that there are two major pillars that support a happy and well rounded life: Family and Faith. I am a devout father and husband of 15 years. I have 7 children, with the oldest now in highschool, while my youngest is so young we still count is age in days. I am also a very devout Christian, and in a world where it seems almost taboo to acknowledge it, I will proudly say that I believe in God, and his son Jesus Christ, and I follow the bible as my compass.

Ken Fisher has lived in the Raleigh Area for the last 15 years.
